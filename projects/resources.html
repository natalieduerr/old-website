about

One of the first projects I was assigned to at OHO Interactive was to work on an Online Resources blog for a retained client. They were a previous client on retainer and with their time for the next few months they wanted us to create this resource platform. I was tasked with creating wireframes and the designs for the listing page and individual pages for the three types of resources: article, video, and infographic. All of the work I produced was overseen by Kelly O’Foran, Visual and UX Designer. The final site will be live soon and this page will be updated with more information and a to view the live site as soon as it launches! If you are interested in viewing my wireframes and designs before the launch, feel free to reach out to me at natalieduerr98@gmail.com and I can share a link to the InVision project!

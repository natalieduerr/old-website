I first became interested in design while working on my high school's yearbook. With very few art classes offered, I started out as a self taught designer. I am now pursuing a BFA in Design, with a concentration in Interaction Design, at Northeastern University, as well as minors in Computer Science and Art History. My goal is to create meaningful interactions and experiences for all kinds of people, all around the world. I’m currently learning how to design and develop accessible websites. When I'm not designing or developing, you can find me reading a good book, watching (and sometimes re-watching) films, playing video games, or hanging with friends!
